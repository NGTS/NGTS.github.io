SCIENCE IMAGES

VLT laser

The VLT laser often crosses our field of view. NGTS has the capability to ignore this laser such that it does not affect our guiding, or the photometry from any stars near it.

Meteors

Occasionally, metoers break up in the earths atmosphere and we can see this with NGTS. These can be difficiult to detect but fortunately only affect a few images. However, they are pretty!

Sattelites

As the number of sattelites in low earth orbit increases, we are seeing an increasing number of sattelites in our images. These can be a real pain, and the brightest ones can seriously affect out tracking.
