Request NGTS observations

To request NGTS observations, please fill in the Request Form.
The more information you provide us, the better. However, if you're unsure about any of the fields, feel free to leave them blank and the operations team will do their best to figure out an appropriate strategy for your observations. Please note that although the form is primarily aimed for exoplanet transit observations we are happy to consider any science case!
If you have any questions please contact us at our email ngtransits@gmail.com or contact our PI Prof. Peter Wheatley and operations manager Jorge Fernández directly at their emails.
