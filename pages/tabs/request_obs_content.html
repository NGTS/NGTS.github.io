Request observations

To request NGTS observations either fill in the form below or email the information required to jorge.fernandez-fernandez.2@warwick.ac.uk or send it to a member of our operations team on slack. The more information you provde us, the better. However, if you're unsure about any of the fields, feel free to leave them blank and the operations team will do their best to figure out an appropriate strategy for your observations. Please note that although the form is primarily aimed for exoplanet transit observations we are happy to consider any science case!
