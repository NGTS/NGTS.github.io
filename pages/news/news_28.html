UKEXOM 2025 - University of Leeds

April 2025: In April 2025, members of the NGTS consortium travelled to the University of Leeds to participate in the UK Exoplanet Meeting (UKEXOM). Consortium members presented posters and talks on a variety of current research projects, from new exoplanet and eclipsing binary discoveries, supporting observations we have provided for external projects and an overview of NGTS' capabilities and what it can do for the UK community. See the gallery page for a full set of NGTS-related posters and talks. The NGTS consortium additionally extends its thanks to the LOC and SOC for putting on a wonderful conference.
News item author: Faith Hawthorn, University of Warwick
