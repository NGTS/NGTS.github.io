NGTS Consortium Meeting 2024 - Geneva

October 2024: In September 2024, members of the NGTS consortium travelled to Geneva Observatory, Switzerland for the next iteration of full-group in-person meetings. Discussion topics included representatives from each of the working groups on recent and upcoming projects, a session for Early-Career Researchers (ECRs) as a new academic year begins, and future synergies with the ESA PLATO mission. Thanks to the Local Organising Committee - Dr Monika Lendl, Dr Matthew Battley, Prof Peter Wheatley.
News item author: Faith Hawthorn, University of Warwick
