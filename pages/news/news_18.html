NGTS discovers ‘dipper’ stars in Orion

May 2023:

‘Dippers’ are a new class of variable star that show large drops in brightness of up to 50%, lasting anywhere from a few hours to a few days. NGTS has led a search of these dipper stars in the Orion Complex using machine learning methods, identifying 120 new dippers - the largest number to be identified in a single cluster to date. The team also determined that around 28 per cent of stars with disks are dipper stars, and all of the dippers in the sample were found to have disks themselves. Read the full journal article.
