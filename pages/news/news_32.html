From Transits to Trends workshop

August 2025: In August of 2025 the city of Albuquerque New Mexico played host to astronomers from the NGTS consortium visiting the University of New Mexico for a workshop titled From Transits to Trends: the Next Decade of Long-Period Exoplanets. The workshop saw members of the scientific community from across the world gather together to discuss and strategise long-period transiting planet science with many contributions from members of the NGTS long-period planet working group. After the workshop had ended a few members of the consortium enjoyed a road-trip across the plains of New Mexico and ended up, among many other (mis)adventures, recording a podcast with some local amateur astronomers which you can listen to here. The consortium would like to offer its congratulations and thanks to the SOC and LOC for organising a very enjoyable and constructive workshop.
News item author: Toby Rodel, Queen's University Belfast
